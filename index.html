Move In Day

On your move in day, we will meet you at the unit to drop off the keys and walk through the unit/building. You will receive the FOB, key to the unit and mailroom. The FOB will give you access into the building, the elvators, mailroom, loading door in the parking ramp and the residential floors. When moving furniture, please use the freight elevator. It is located on the left side of the elevator bay. Please use the freight elevator to also transport your pets. Each floor has lime green grocery carts. Please return after use. Have your movers park in the loading area located on St. Peter Street. We suggest moving on the weekend as utility vechicles often occupy this space during the week.
